Accepting Gift Cards

Accept gift cards using the Square Payment Form.

https://developer.squareup.com/docs/payment-form/gift-cards/part-1

Important: If you are adding Gift Cards to a payment page that already uses an instance of the Square Payment Form for credit cards, you must initialize a second Square Payment Form for the gift cards. How to Render Multiple Payment Forms

Prerequisites

Follow steps 1-5 of the Payment Form Setup Guide before continuing.

6. Add the gift card fields

import { GiftCardInput, CreditCardSubmitButton } from 'react-square-payment-form';

<SquarePaymentForm {...props}>
  <GiftCardInput />
  <CreditCardSubmitButton>
    Pay with Gift Card
  </CreditCardSubmitButton>
</SquarePaymentForm>

7. Update the callback function

Modify the cardNonceResponseReceived callback to handle your gift card logic and send the nonce to your backend.

8. Generate a nonce

You can use the Gift Card test card in Sandbox to verify that the form is generating a nonce.
